About Me

I'm passionate about transforming power infrastructure through digital innovation. My journey began in Iraq's national grid systems, where I witnessed firsthand the challenges of aging infrastructure. I'm particularly interested in the intersection of power engineering with data analytics, where predictive maintenance and real-time monitoring can revolutionize grid management.
